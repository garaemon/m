## Multiple lines
Please use `\begin{eqnarray} ... \end{eqnarray}` to render equation with multiple line.
```mathjax
\begin{eqnarray}
& & {}^\forall \varepsilon \gt 0, {}^\exists \delta \gt 0 \mbox{ s.t. } \\
& & {}^\forall x \in \mathbb{ R }, 0 \lt |x - a| \lt \delta
\Rightarrow |f(x) - b| \lt \varepsilon
\end{eqnarray}
```
